# Product Overview

Shuren is an AI-powered personal fitness application that acts as a digital personal trainer, nutritionist, and coach. The system uses specialized AI agents to provide structured fitness guidance with flexibility.

## Core Value Proposition

- Personalized workout and meal plans tailored to individual fitness levels and goals
- Adaptive execution that adjusts to real life without guilt or punishment
- Context-aware conversational assistance that understands user's current state
- Fixed plans with flexible execution - structure stays consistent, but adapts when life happens

## Target Users

- Fitness beginners, intermediate, and advanced users
- Users with goals: fat loss, muscle gain, or general fitness
- Anyone seeking structured guidance without expensive personal trainers

## Key Features

1. **Smart Onboarding**: One-time mandatory setup capturing fitness level, goals, diet preferences, workout schedule, and meal timing
2. **AI Agent Team**: Six specialized agents (Workout Planning, Diet Planning, Supplement Guidance, Tracking & Adjustment, Scheduling & Reminder, Conversational Assistant)
3. **Visual Workout Guidance**: GIF-based exercise demonstrations
4. **Adaptive Tracking**: Adjusts future plans based on missed sessions without penalizing progress
5. **Context-Aware Chat**: Understands where user is in their journey (mid-workout, meal prep, etc.)

## Design Principles

- **Onboarding-driven**: Most configurations captured once and reused
- **Fixed plans, flexible execution**: Plans are stable unless explicitly changed
- **Guidance-only**: No medical advice or diagnosis
- **No guilt**: System adapts to reality without punishment
- **Explainable**: Users understand why suggestions are made

## Boundaries

- NOT a medical device - provides fitness guidance only
- NOT a replacement for doctors or healthcare professionals
- NOT a rigid rule engine - flexible and understanding of real life
